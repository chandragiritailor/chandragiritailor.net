Services

A tailor shop provides an essential range of services centered on garment alteration and custom creation, ensuring clothes fit perfectly and last longer. The primary offerings include Alterations and Repairs, which involve modifying ready-to-wear clothing by hemming pants and sleeves, adjusting waistlines, tapering seams for a slimmer profile, and performing intricate work on formalwear like wedding dresses. They also handle necessary repairs, such as replacing zippers, mending tears, and reinforcing worn fabrics.
